Exam Management Help

Exam Management is used to create MCQ exams for a selected academic year, assign them to the correct batch/division and subject, and track student submissions.

  • New Exam sets the exam title, batch/division, subject, date, duration, and publish status.
  • Draft exams are for preparation. Published exams are ready for students to attempt.
  • Questions must be added separately before students can properly attempt the exam.
  • Submissions show student attempts and help staff review exam participation and results.
  • Filters help find exams by batch/division, status, title, or subject.
Important: verify academic year, batch/division, subject, exam date, duration, marks, and questions before publishing.

  • New Exam मध्ये exam title, batch/division, subject, date, duration आणि publish status set केला जातो.
  • Draft exams preparation साठी असतात. Published exams students attempt करण्यासाठी ready असतात.
  • Questions students नी exam properly attempt करण्यापूर्वी separately add करणे आवश्यक आहे.
  • Submissions student attempts दाखवतात आणि exam participation/results review करण्यासाठी मदत करतात.
  • Filters वापरून batch/division, status, title किंवा subject नुसार exams शोधता येतात.
