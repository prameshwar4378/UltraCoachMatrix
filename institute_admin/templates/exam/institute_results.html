Exam Results Help

Exam Results shows submitted online exam attempts with score, percentage, rank, and performance status.

  • Filters help review results by batch/division, course, subject, exam, student, performance, percentage, and submission date.
  • Ranks are calculated separately within each exam. Students with equal scores receive the same rank.
  • Performance separates passed students from students who need improvement based on the configured result rule.
  • Correct, Wrong, and Unattempted counts help teachers understand question-level attempt quality.
  • Excel and PDF exports are useful for academic review, parent meetings, and office records.
Important: results are shown only for submitted attempts and depend on the correct answers and marks set in Exam Questions.
