Expense Management Help

Expense Management is used to record and monitor money spent by the institute, so office accounts stay clear and monthly spending can be reviewed properly.

  • Use this page whenever institute money is spent on salary advances, rent, electricity, repairs, stationery, printing, transport, events, material purchase, or other operational costs.
  • Add Expense should be used on the same day or as soon as possible, with the correct title, amount, spent date, note, and supporting document if available.
  • Filters help review expenses by title, note, recorded user, and date range for daily, monthly, or audit checks.
  • Stats show total spending, this month's spending, and filtered amount so management can compare income and outgoing cash.
  • Export is useful for accountant review, owner reporting, audit records, and offline backup.
Important: verify amount, date, purpose, and proof before saving. Avoid deleting expense records unless the entry was created by mistake.
