Notice & App Notification Center Help

Notice & App Notification Center is used to create official institute announcements and prepare them for student/parent mobile app visibility.

  • Use this page for holidays, exam updates, fee reminders, events, timetable changes, emergency messages, and general institute communication.
  • Audience decides who should see the notice, such as all users, selected batches/divisions, courses, students, teachers, or parents.
  • Schedule helps publish a notice immediately, keep it as draft, plan it for a future date, or expire it after a fixed time.
  • Push to App marks the notice for student/parent Android app notification and mobile notice display where app integration is available.
  • Read count helps staff understand whether students or parents have opened the notice.
Important: verify title, message, audience, publish time, expiry time, and app option before publishing important notices.

Notice & App Notification Center institute च्या official announcements तयार करण्यासाठी आणि student/parent mobile app वर दाखवण्यासाठी वापरले जाते.

  • हे page वापरा holidays, exam updates, fee reminders, events, timetable changes, emergency messages आणि general institute communication साठी.
  • Audience notice कोणाला दिसेल हे ठरवते, जसे all users, selected batches/divisions, courses, students, teachers किंवा parents.
  • Schedule notice immediately publish करणे, draft ठेवणे, future date साठी plan करणे किंवा fixed time नंतर expire करणे यासाठी मदत करते.
  • Push to App notice student/parent Android app notification आणि mobile notice display साठी mark करते, जिथे app integration available आहे.
  • Read count students किंवा parents ने notice open केला आहे का हे staff ला समजण्यास मदत करते.
Important: important notices publish करण्यापूर्वी title, message, audience, publish time, expiry time आणि app option verify करा.
