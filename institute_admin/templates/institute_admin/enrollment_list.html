Enrollment Management Help

Enrollment Management connects a student to the correct course/class and batch/division for the selected academic year. This is the bridge between admission, academic work, and fee calculation.

  • New Enrollment is used when a student must be assigned to a class/course and division like 10th-A or 10th-B.
  • Courses decide the course-wise fee total unless a custom fee is entered.
  • Batch/Division decides where the student appears for attendance, homework, exams, reports, and teacher assignment.
  • Status shows whether the enrollment is active, completed, or cancelled.
  • Filters help find records by student, batch/division, course/class, or status.
Important: verify student, course/class, batch/division, and fee before saving. Wrong enrollment can affect fees, attendance, exams, homework, and reports.

Enrollment Management selected academic year साठी student ला योग्य course/class आणि batch/division सोबत connect करते. हे admission, academic work आणि fee calculation यांना जोडणारा main link आहे.

  • New Enrollment student ला 10th-A किंवा 10th-B सारख्या class/course आणि division मध्ये assign करण्यासाठी वापरले जाते.
  • Courses course-wise fee total ठरवतात, custom fee दिली असल्यास ती वापरली जाते.
  • Batch/Division student attendance, homework, exams, reports आणि teacher assignment मध्ये कुठे दिसेल ते ठरवते.
  • Status enrollment active, completed किंवा cancelled आहे का ते दाखवतो.
  • Filters वापरून student, batch/division, course/class किंवा status नुसार records शोधता येतात.
Important: save करण्यापूर्वी student, course/class, batch/division आणि fee verify करा. चुकीचा enrollment fees, attendance, exams, homework आणि reports वर परिणाम करू शकतो.

Enrollment Management selected academic year के लिए student को सही course/class और batch/division से connect करता है. यह admission, academic work और fee calculation के बीच main link है.

  • New Enrollment student को 10th-A या 10th-B जैसी class/course और division में assign करने के लिए उपयोग होता है.
  • Courses course-wise fee total तय करते हैं, जब तक custom fee entered न हो.
  • Batch/Division तय करता है कि student attendance, homework, exams, reports और teacher assignment में कहां दिखाई देगा.
  • Status enrollment active, completed या cancelled है, यह दिखाता है.
