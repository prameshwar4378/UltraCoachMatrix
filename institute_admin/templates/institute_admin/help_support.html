Before You Submit

This information helps the support team resolve the issue faster.

  1. Refresh the page and try the action once more.
  2. Confirm the correct academic year, course, batch, or student is selected.
  3. Write the page name and the exact message shown on screen.
  4. Explain what you clicked and what result you expected.
  5. Do not include passwords, OTPs, bank PINs, or other secret information.
