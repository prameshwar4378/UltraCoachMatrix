Student Admissions Help

Student Admissions is the main page for maintaining every admitted student's academic and account record. Use it after a student joins the institute or when an existing student's admission, parent, class, batch/division, or fee information needs attention.

  • New Student is used for a fresh admission. It stores student details, parent/guardian contact, login details, admission number, selected class/course, batch/division, and fee setup.
  • Student Dashboard opens the complete student profile, including admission details, enrollments, fee records, documents, certificates, and related academic information.
  • Enroll is used when a student must be added to a course/class and batch/division for the selected academic year.
  • Fee Summary helps you quickly understand total fees, paid amount, and pending dues before follow-up or payment collection.
  • Import and Export are useful for bulk admissions, reporting, data checking, and sharing selected student fields with management.
  • Edit or Delete should be used carefully because student records are connected with fees, enrollments, documents, certificates, and academic history.
Important: before deleting or changing a student, verify the selected academic year, batch/division, fee dues, and parent contact details.
