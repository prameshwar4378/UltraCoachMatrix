Subject Management Help

Subject Management is used to create and maintain the subjects taught in your institute. These subjects are used while preparing exams, homework, academic activities, and reports.

  • Total Subjects, Active Subjects, and Inactive Subjects show a quick summary of your subject setup.
  • Filter Subjects helps you find subjects by status or subject name.
  • New Subject opens the subject form where you can add the subject name, description, and active status.
  • Subject Records displays the subjects for the selected academic year.
  • Edit and Delete actions help you maintain existing subject data.

Subject Management चा वापर आपल्या संस्थेत शिकवले जाणारे subjects तयार आणि व्यवस्थापित करण्यासाठी केला जातो. हे subjects exams, homework, academic activities आणि reports साठी वापरले जातात.

  • Total Subjects, Active Subjects आणि Inactive Subjects मधून subject setup चा झटपट आढावा मिळतो.
  • Filter Subjects वापरून status किंवा subject name नुसार subjects शोधता येतात.
  • New Subject मधून subject name, description आणि active status भरून नवीन subject तयार करता येतो.
  • Subject Records मध्ये selected academic year चे subjects दिसतात.
  • Edit आणि Delete actions वापरून existing subject data update किंवा remove करता येतो.

Subject Management का उपयोग आपके institute में पढ़ाए जाने वाले subjects बनाने और manage करने के लिए किया जाता है. ये subjects exams, homework, academic activities और reports में उपयोग होते हैं.

  • Total Subjects, Active Subjects और Inactive Subjects आपके subject setup का quick summary दिखाते हैं.
  • Filter Subjects से आप status या subject name के अनुसार subjects खोज सकते हैं.
  • New Subject से subject name, description और active status भरकर नया subject बनाया जा सकता है.
  • Subject Records selected academic year के subjects दिखाता है.
  • Edit और Delete actions से existing subject data को update या remove किया जा सकता है.
