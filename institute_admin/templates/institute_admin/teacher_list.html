Teacher Management Help

Teacher Management is used to create and maintain teacher profiles and login access for academic work inside the institute.

  • Add a teacher when a new teacher joins or when an existing staff member needs teacher access.
  • Teacher details store contact information, employee ID, qualification, specialization, teacher type, and workload limits.
  • Why it matters: teachers can be assigned to batches/divisions and can manage academic work such as attendance, homework, exams, and results based on access.
  • Update records when contact details, specialization, employment type, or workload changes.
  • Deactivate/delete carefully when a teacher leaves or should no longer access institute data.

Teacher Management चा वापर institute मधील academic work साठी teacher profiles आणि login access तयार व maintain करण्यासाठी केला जातो.

  • Teacher add करा जेव्हा नवीन teacher join होतो किंवा existing staff ला teacher access लागतो.
  • Teacher details मध्ये contact information, employee ID, qualification, specialization, teacher type आणि workload limits ठेवले जातात.
  • हे का महत्त्वाचे आहे: teachers ना batches/divisions assign करता येतात आणि access नुसार attendance, homework, exams आणि results manage करता येतात.
  • Records update करा contact details, specialization, employment type किंवा workload बदलल्यावर.
  • Deactivate/delete काळजीपूर्वक करा teacher institute सोडल्यावर किंवा institute data access नको असल्यावर.

Teacher Management का उपयोग institute के academic work के लिए teacher profiles और login access बनाने और maintain करने के लिए किया जाता है.

  • Teacher add करें जब नया teacher join हो या existing staff को teacher access चाहिए.
  • Teacher details में contact information, employee ID, qualification, specialization, teacher type और workload limits रखे जाते हैं.
  • यह क्यों जरूरी है: teachers को batches/divisions assign किए जा सकते हैं और access के अनुसार attendance, homework, exams और results manage किए जा सकते हैं.
  • Records update करें जब contact details, specialization, employment type या workload बदलता है.
  • Deactivate/delete सावधानी से करें जब teacher institute छोड़ दे या institute data access नहीं चाहिए.
