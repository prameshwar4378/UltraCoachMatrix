Attendance Control Desk Help

Attendance Control Desk is used to mark and review daily attendance for students in a selected batch/division and date.

  • Select Batch and Date first. Attendance is saved only for that selected batch/division and day.
  • Present, Absent, and Late should be marked carefully because these values affect student reports and attendance percentage.
  • Quick Mark helps mark all visible students at once, but review exceptions before saving.
  • Notes should be used only for important reasons, such as late reason, approved leave, or correction detail.
  • Export Records creates attendance reports for parent meetings, office review, and monthly reporting.
Important: always confirm the date, batch/division, and student list before clicking Save Attendance.

Attendance Control Desk selected batch/division आणि date साठी students ची daily attendance mark आणि review करण्यासाठी वापरले जाते.

  • Batch आणि Date आधी select करा. Attendance फक्त selected batch/division आणि त्या day साठी save होते.
  • Present, Absent आणि Late काळजीपूर्वक mark करा, कारण याचा student reports आणि attendance percentage वर परिणाम होतो.
  • Quick Mark visible students एकाच वेळी mark करण्यासाठी मदत करते, पण save करण्यापूर्वी exceptions तपासा.
  • Notes फक्त important reasons साठी वापरा, जसे late reason, approved leave किंवा correction detail.
  • Export Records parent meetings, office review आणि monthly reporting साठी attendance reports तयार करते.
Important: Save Attendance क्लिक करण्यापूर्वी date, batch/division आणि student list नेहमी confirm करा.

  • Notes केवल important reasons के लिए उपयोग करें, जैसे late reason, approved leave या correction detail.
  • Export Records parent meetings, office review और monthly reporting के लिए attendance reports बनाता है.
